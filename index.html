About Solaada

Reliable power, built for Ghana's sun — and Ghana's grid.

Solaada was founded to give homes and businesses in Weija Junction and beyond a dependable alternative to an unpredictable grid. We size, design, and install solar systems using the abundant sunlight in the Greater Accra Region, paired with battery storage that keeps essentials running through outages.

From a single rooftop system for a family home to larger arrays for shops, schools, and offices, our team handles site assessment, system design, installation, and after-sales maintenance — all from our base at Weija Junction.

Our promise: the sun does the collecting during the day; your battery does the delivering at night. That's the whole idea behind Solaada.

Solar solutions for every roof and every budget
